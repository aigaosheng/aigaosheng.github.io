This is No. 45, Emerald hill road in Singapore, a quiet and historical place, located in the busy orchard road, near 313 summerset. The Chinese style building is built in 1903 and awarded architectural heritage in 2001. Among the area, there are restaurants, bars and residents. You cannot imagine a few hundreds meters way, it is crowded and busy orchard road.

History is kept for memory and understanding how old times living. Although around 200 years, the building looks better reserved.
