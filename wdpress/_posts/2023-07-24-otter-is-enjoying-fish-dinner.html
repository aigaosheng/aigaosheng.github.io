Everyday is same, cycling from office to home along Singapore river. I know near jiak Kim bridge a family of otters living there. Sometimes I saw them resting on the grass of river bank.

Today is special. It is first time to see them catching fish and enjoying fish dinner. Fish is so big and fatty. They are catching fish and climbing stone step from river to land.

Enjoying peaceful nature.
