Time flies so fast. It is 2024 already. Climbing bukit timah hill is a traditional celebration event of new year together with friends. The 2024 first day is great for hiking, a little cloud, not hot as usual. Bukit Timah is a popular site for Singaporean, car park always full. Group of people come up and down, with family or friends. The hill is only about 160 meters, suitable for kids and old. The way is easy, except a short steep. There are multiple ways to reach hill, some easy, and some a little difficulty. The following is a created video for celebrating the first day 2024. Best wishes for the friends in the world.
