There is almost half-year I do not cycling to western in Singapore. On this Sunday afternoon, go cycling to Pandan reservoir to watch sunset, a great place in Singapore.

From Alexandra, enter into railway green road near AIA building. Then turn into Ulu panda river from Buona vista mrt. There is few people along the river road. Thus great for cycling. Along the river, cross Boon Lay way and go direction to jurong lake garden along the cycling road under mrt line. But there is too many traffic light to waiting. So back to pandan reservoir for sunset viewing.

The water area of Pandan reservoir is quite large, open and the hiking road around the reservoir more than 5 km.
